Wishes for You

A letter of thought, hope, and devotion.

Dear Ulfa,

Your presence in my life has been a constant source of joy and inspiration. As I sit down to write this, I feel surrounded by the memories we have created together.

i want every single morning light to hit your eyes exactly like that first time i saw you. when life gets heavy, i’m right there, step for step. and i need you to see yourself the way i see you: resilient, brilliant, deeply loved. period.

We’ve been through some rough patches and had our wins up on them sunny highs. Through it all, you’ve stayed solid, and that’s been my anchor. I promise I’ma keep exploring the world with you, keep finding new reasons to respect you, and remind you every day just how much you matter.

Your future’s about to be just as lush and layered as everything we’ve already built. we’re growing side by side, roots tangled so deep we’re unshakeable. let’s get a hundred more wild adventures, a thousand more quiet, nothing-else-matters moments, and a whole lifetime of shared dreams colliding.

Sincerely, Rasend
